FAQ

Admissions Questions.

Still have questions? Our admissions office is open Monday–Friday, 9am to 5pm. Call +92 21 111 0000 or email admissions@crestwood.edu.pk

What are the minimum entry requirements for undergraduate admission? +
Most undergraduate programmes require a minimum of 60% in FSc/A-Levels or equivalent, with relevant subject pre-requisites. Engineering and Medical programmes have higher requirements (75%+). Specific requirements are listed on each programme's page. We also consider extracurricular achievements and the personal statement.
Does Crestwood offer scholarships and financial aid? +
Yes. We offer four scholarship categories: Merit Scholarships (top 10% of intake), Need-Based Bursaries (assessed via financial means test), Athletic Scholarships (for national-level athletes), and Talent Awards (for exceptional performance in arts, debate, or science olympiads). Approximately 40% of students receive some form of financial support.
Is hostel accommodation available on campus? +
Yes. We have separate hostels for male and female students, accommodating approximately 2,800 students in total. Rooms are allocated on a first-come, first-served basis after admission confirmation. We also maintain a list of verified off-campus housing near the university.
Are international students eligible to apply? +
Yes. Crestwood welcomes students from all countries. International applicants must provide equivalency certificates from the Inter-Board Committee of Chairmen (IBCC) and may need to demonstrate English proficiency via IELTS (minimum 6.5) or TOEFL (minimum 85). Special international student support services are available including visa assistance.
Can I transfer to Crestwood from another university? +
Transfer admissions are considered for students who have completed at least one full year at a recognised institution with a CGPA of 2.5 or above. Credit transfer is evaluated case by case by the relevant department. Transfer applications are accepted in limited numbers each intake — early application is strongly advised.
